Ramen Koika - Logo Design + Concept Development

A new Japanese noodle restaurant on Davie St, downtown Vancouver, BC.
Ramen Koika asked a new logo design with story telling along with total design concept of the restaurant interior include signage. ‘A Song of Love’, meaning of Koika, Japanese and Korean symbol letters harmonized in the bowl of brush stroke. Modern but bold logo gives authentic brand value and recognize quickly in the local noodle restaurants.
